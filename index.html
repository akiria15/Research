The Evolution of Headphones in Five Stages

What are headphones, and what is their purpose?

Headphones are similar to speakers, but made for personal use. Headphones chaged the dynamic of many daily lives. Everywhere we go there are people that own a pair. You can see them in schools, libraries, stores, travelling, and any other public place because they are very useful. They come in so many styles and colors. The purpose of headphones is to give people a chance so listen to things audibly without disturbing anyone else or just so they can have their privacy. They are designed for the human ear and some have added foam to make them comfortable. They have a cord or cable to connect to any electronic device in order for the audio to transfer.

Milestone 1: The 1895's

The image above is the first headphones that were advertised. The headphones of this time were invented for commercial and to be used by people. They did not fully succeed because they were so expensive. Not many people bought them or were able to afford them. Many designs and prototypes were created. The most popular and known design was similar to the look of a stethoscope. The headphones were said to give people the opportunity to hear songs from the opera through them. Everyone wanted that experience, many people just were not priveledged enough to get that.

Milestone 2: The 1949's

Above is an image of this era of headphones. The headphones of this time were created by AKG. Unlike the fist headphones, these wre a little less expensive due to them not being products of opera music. The purpose and goal of these being created was to have a different pproach in the use of headphones. These were seen to be used for sound and not specifically art like mmusic. The intentions of these were to hear things privately or better access to diffeent things that people enjoy listening to.

Milestone 3: The 1979's

Above is an image that show the headphones created by Sony. The headphones of this time held great value. They came with metal headbands and they were lightweight. As they hit the industry, it was very noticeable that they were a big change from the most recently known headphones. These headphones molded the design for others that came out during this time. No value can be placed because they were the first to change the whole look and meaning of headphones.

Milestone 4: The 2000's

Here is an image of the QC1. As time progressed from the last change in headphones, these were invented and used for noise cancelling. Originally used by pilots and passengers on the plane along with them. The objective goalof these were noise cancellation, which explains why they were used mostly by that audience of people. They began to be shown more in the market and people were able to purchase them for more personal use.

The most recent Milestone: 2016

The image above is the most recent headphones that were created, some people like to call them earbuds. Overall the meaning is the same. These are the most modern and popular since the invention of headphones. Having these changed the whole dynamic of the way people enjoyed music and audio. The price is as low as $100. These bluetooth wireless headphones were created for an easier way of listening to diffeent things.
